# Faculty Clearance Workflow - Quick Reference Card 🎯

**Print this card or bookmark it for quick access during clearance processing**

---

## 📋 The 12 Departments (In Order)

```
PHASE 1: Physical Items
1️⃣  Library      → Books returned? Fines paid?
2️⃣  Lab          → Equipment verified? Safety done?
3️⃣  Pharmacy     → Stocks counted? Access removed?

PHASE 2: Financial & Admin
4️⃣  HR           → Salary final? Leaves cleared?
5️⃣  Finance      → Dues paid? No charges?
6️⃣  Records      → Grades in? Thesis submitted?

PHASE 3: Operations
7️⃣  IT           → Systems deactivated
8️⃣  Admin        → Keys returned? Access removed?
9️⃣  ORIC         → Research closed? Papers done?

PHASE 4: Final Authority
🔟 Warden       → Hostel clear (if applicable)
1️⃣1️⃣ HOD         → Reviews ALL 9 above ⭐
1️⃣2️⃣ Dean        → FINAL SIGNATURE ⭐
```

---

---

## 🚨 Critical Rules

1. **HOD cannot sign until all 9 departments (Phase 1-3) approved**
   - Cannot skip any
   - Cannot reorder

2. **Dean is final - absolutely last**
   - Can only sign after HOD
   - Dean signature = clearance complete

3. **No backtracking**
   - Once approved, assume complete
   - If issue later, new clearance needed

4. **Finance is key**
   - Get Finance approval early
   - Other depts sometimes wait for it

## 💡 Pro Tips

✅ **Start day 1**: Submit clearance request  
✅ **Do Phase 1 first**: Quick wins  
✅ **Contact Finance early**: Busiest department  
✅ **Call ahead**: Confirm availability before visiting  
✅ **Bring copies**: Get photos of each approval  
✅ **Make checklist**: Cross off as you go  
✅ **Track dates**: Note when each dept signs  
✅ **Keep phone**: Department may call with questions  
✅ **Bundle visits**: Do nearby depts together  
✅ **Save originals**: Keep all forms for records

## ⚠️ If You Get Rejected

```
Example: Finance rejects (fees not paid)

Step 1: Read the rejection reason
        "Outstanding tuition fee of $2,000"

Step 2: Fix the issue
        Pay the fee, get receipt

Step 3: Resubmit to Finance
        Bring receipt, request re-approval

Step 4: Wait for re-processing (1-2 days)

Step 5: Once approved, resume other depts

Timeline impact: +2-3 days
```
